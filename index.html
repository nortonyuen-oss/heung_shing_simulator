City Builder — How to Play

🏗 Building Your City

🛣 Roads ($10) — Connect zones; roads are required for growth 🟩 Zone R ($50) — Residential: citizens live here 🟦 Zone C ($60) — Commercial: shops & offices 🟨 Zone I ($50) — Industrial: factories ⚡ Power Line ($5) — Carry electricity across tiles 🏭 Coal Plant ($3,000) — Generates power; causes pollution 🌞 Solar Plant ($6,000) — Clean power, no pollution 🚒 Fire Station ($1,000) — Fire protection; boosts happiness 👮 Police ($800) — Reduces crime; boosts happiness 🌳 Parks — Choose open ground, playground, or large 3×3 park; boosts nearby residential happiness 🔨 Bulldoze ($5) — Remove buildings, zones, roads

💡 Growth Rules

• Zones must be adjacent to a road to grow • Power makes zones grow 5× faster (unpowered zones still grow at 20% rate) • Balance R / C / I demand — watch the bars in the top-right HUD • High taxes suppress residential demand • Fire stations & police improve happiness, which boosts all demand • Parks make nearby residential areas happier and more valuable

⌨ Controls

Left-drag Paint selected tool Right-drag Pan the camera Scroll Zoom in / out Hover tile Debug tooltip shows growth conditions Space Pause / unpause simulation Ctrl+S Save game Ctrl+O Load game (open saves list)
